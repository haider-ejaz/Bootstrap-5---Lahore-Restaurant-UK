Welcome To Lahore
Restaurant

Your Favourite Pakistani Restaurant in Watford

The Lahore Restaurant brings the taste of your Hometown in Watford. For the past 20 years, we have been striving to provide you with authentic Pakistani Cuisine that is 100% Halal and will be a treat for your tastebuds.

Whether you are looking for an affordable halal restaurant that delivers delicious meals to your doorsteps or want to try the famous Pakistani cuisine near you, we have you covered!

With an inclusive, welcoming atmosphere, you can get it delivered online or dine-in at the Lahore Restaurant any day of the week and enjoy it with your friends & family. Choose from a variety of lavish dishes, drinks, and starters on our extensive menu, and we will create those for you from scratch with love.

What makes us the best Indian & Pakistani Restaurant in Watford


The Lahore Restaurant provides its customers with the most authentic meals from Pakistan and India. We are not only proud of the original taste & culture we offer but also the variety of non-alcoholic drinks, vegetables sourced from organic sources, and 100% halal meat used in our dishes.

The blend of Indian, Far and Middle Eastern cooking techniques, a variety of rich spices, and juicy meat give our items complex flavours you have never tasted before.

Together, these factors complement our traditional values. The wonderful ambience, exemplary service, mouth-watering food, and exceptional dining experience make us the best halal restaurant with Pakistani cuisine in Watford, UK.
